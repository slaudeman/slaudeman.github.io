About me

My name is Sara Laudeman. I am an architectural designer with a passion for sustainability and an insatiable curiousity. I hold a professional Master of Architecture from Georgia Tech. I hold undergraduate degrees in architecture and mathematics from the University of North Carolina - Charlotte.
