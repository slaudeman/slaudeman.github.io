Selected Projects

cove.tool

Carbon Feature

Role - Product Manager

The Carbon Feature is an early-stage carbon estimation tool within the cove.tool platoform. It provides users with an intuitive building carbon accounting tool to support their project pursuits. The tool integrates data from the Embodied Carbon in Construction Calculator (EC3) and Carbon Leadership Forum (CLF) standards in order to provide users with automated takeoffs, Environmental Product Declaration (EPD) resources, and results.

Materials and Assemblies Database

Role - Product/Project Manager

Working with diverse internal and external teams, the project team defined, developed, and populated two paired databases focused on materials, specifications, assembly, and production data in order to support users in defining their building construction methods, accounting for embodied carbon and thermal performance, and supporting manufacturer integrations within the cove.tool platform. This project required deep knowledge of specifications, product information including cost and carbon, and construction methods.
